### Executive Summary

### Activity & Responsiveness

- Average and median response times
- % responses under 1 hour vs over 1 hour

- State transition analysis — where are leads advancing vs dropping off?

- Submissions sent, offer rate, decline rate
- Total offer volume and funded volume
- Decline reason patterns — are there recurring issues?

### AI Collaboration
- Ratio of AI vs human-sent messages
- Analysis of whether AI is doing the heavy lifting or the broker is engaged

### Current Pipeline Health
- Pipeline snapshot by state
- Flag anything concerning (too many leads stuck, empty stages, etc.)

### Verdict & Recommendations
- 2-3 specific, actionable recommendations for this broker
- Be honest — if performance is poor, say so diplomatically but clearly
- If performance is strong, acknowledge it and suggest how to level up

**RULES:**
- Be data-driven. Every claim must reference a number.
- Compare to team averages wherever possible.
- Tone: professional analyst. Direct, not sugar-coated.

- Round percentages to 1 decimal place.
